How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice softening from day 3, with full effect at 10–14 days. A complimentary review/top‑up is available if clinically indicated.

No. We prioritise movement and nuance. Our approach preserves your natural expressions while easing the lines that age you.

About

Clinical excellence with an artist’s eye.

I began my nursing journey at Oxford Brookes University in 2000, where I honed my skills in various London hospitals, specialising in A&E and Paediatric Intensive Care.

Transitioning from critical care, I delved into the world of aesthetic medicine, finding my niche at a prestigious Harley Street clinic. There, I cultivated expertise in facial aesthetics, driven by a passion for helping individuals feel confident in their skin.

In 2016, I pursued further education, obtaining my Masters to become an Advanced Nurse Practitioner. Now, I operate autonomously in a dynamic GP clinic, catering to diverse patient needs, from conducting thorough assessments to prescribing treatments for a spectrum of conditions.

With over 25 years of nursing experience, I pride myself on making evidence-based decisions, ensuring the highest quality care for my patients. I am committed to employing only the finest products and adhering strictly to evidence-based practices.

My approach is personalized to each client, aiming to enhance their natural beauty and instil a sense of self-assurance. I strive to provide a professional, empathetic, and tailored experience to all, fostering a positive transformation in both appearance and confidence.
